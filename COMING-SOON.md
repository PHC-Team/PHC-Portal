# 🚧 Coming Soon

PHC-Portal is actively being developed, with new features, guides, and improvements on the way. Here's a preview of what's currently in development.

---

## 📖 Hypervisor Guide

Comprehensive documentation covering Hypervisor-based Denuvo bypasses. The guide will include step-by-step Hypervisor setup instructions, troubleshooting tips, frequently asked questions, best practices, and information on obtaining the required Hypervisor files.

---

## 📖 Denuvo Guide

A complete guide for Denuvo-protected games that use a bypass without requiring a Hypervisor. It will explain how to obtain the necessary bypass files, how to use them correctly, answer common questions, and provide an overview of how the bypass process works.

---

## 🛠 Fixes Guides

A growing collection of troubleshooting guides for common issues that may occur when using Steam manifests. These guides will cover problems such as games not appearing in your Steam library, **No Internet Connection** errors, download issues, and other common problems to help you get back up and running quickly.
